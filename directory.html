This is NYC Icarus central. Our local community supports both organizing and peer-support.

Please sign up for our low-traffic, announcement only mailing list:

https://lists.riseup.net/www/subscribe/icarusnyc-events

The riseup list software is very, err, protective. You may need to visit https://lists.riseup.net/ and then search for 'nycicarus' to find the link to subscribe to "NYC Icarus announcements list". Sometimes the direct link fails.

You can also request to join our NYC organizers mailing list:

https://lists.riseup.net/www/admin/nycicarus-organizers

This is our Facebook Group (although we try to minimize the organizing and conversations that happen there):

https://www.facebook.com/IcarusProjectNewYorkCity

Follow us on twitter: @nycicarus

You can contact nycicarus at gmail dot com if you have any questions.
